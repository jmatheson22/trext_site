Trext is software that lets you engage by text at scale.




We call our SMS conversations Trees because they are branched. Different users get different texts based on what they say. This means an end user never has to see an irrelevant text.


Every conversation is stored. Trext is great for surveys, polls, signups and more. Export to Excel in just one click.


Batch texts may be sent directly to your contact lists. They are useful for calls to action, rapid data collection, or alerts.
