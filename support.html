Software that engages customers in interactive texting




Step 1: Design support for your product

Create a decision tree by walking customers through their support issues. Trext uses a branching logic that can automatically respond differently to different users.


Step 2: Send your texts

Publishing your tree so users can text in--just provide a QR code or phone number. Trext allows for a personal interactive conversation with thousands of people at once.


Step 3: Sit back, get data

All responses are stored and exported to Excel in just one click, allowing for powerful analytics. Record how the support went and join the conversation anytime!*
